## Patterns adapted into original material

- A core spine with optional specialization rather than one enormous linear course.
- Outcomes before commands.
- Small mental models connected to observable state.
- Safety labels and prerequisite checks before mutation.
- Progressive hints that preserve learner agency.
- A failure rhythm of symptom, signal, cause, controlled fix, verification, and prevention.
- Cleanup as part of completion.
- Production context and honest limitation language.
- Reflection based on what the learner actually observed and verified.

General Kubernetes, Helm, Ansible, GitOps, certification, industrial-domain, and live-cloud content was deliberately excluded from this adaptation.
